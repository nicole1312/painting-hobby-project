Art Painting

" Art is the only way to run away without leaving home."
Twyla Tharp

Have you been considering learning to paint? Painting isn't just fun, it also provides a variety of health benefits. From sparking your own creative grownth to unlocking better mental and emotional wellness, here are some of the benefits of painting you don't want to miss.

  • ◎ Painting promotes creative growth;
  • ◎ Enhances problem-solving skills;
  • ◎ Improves fine motor skills;
  • ◎ Encourages positivity and offers stress relief;
  • ◎ Boosts self-confidence;
  • ◎ Painting improves memory, concentration and spatial awareness;
  • ◎ Stimulates emotional awareness and growth;
  • ◎ Offers a healthy challenge;
  • ◎ Painting builds nonverbal communication skills.
